A New Way to Pay With Your PNC Visa Cards

There’s an easier way to pay online. With Visa's new online checkout experience you can use your PNC Visa card to make purchases so there is no need to re-enter your information with every online purchase. Just select to pay with your PNC Visa card when you see the Click to Pay icon  where Visa is accepted.

Whether you're shopping on your computer, mobile device, or in-app, when you select the payment icon, you can quickly complete your purchase in a few steps. When you use your PNC card where you see the Click to Pay icon  you'll get the same benefits and rewards as when you tap, swipe or insert you card!

To Get Started

Log in to the PNC Mobile App[1] or PNC Online Banking.

Navigate to your card and look for the Click to Pay icon  - Select the cards you want to add and enter your email address. If you’re an existing Visa Checkout user, you’ll have access to the PNC cards you already stored.

Select to pay with your PNC Visa card when you see the Click to Pay icon  where Visa is accepted.
