Make a time-consuming task easier.

Pay your bills without the hassles of writing checks or remembering user names and passwords at multiple biller sites.

Simply sign on, tell us who you want to pay, enter the payment amount, set the payment date, and submit. You will still have the ability to update or cancel your payment anytime before it processes. Best of all, it's FREE![2]

  • Schedule your bills in just minutes
  • Pay individuals and businesses of all sizes

Between Your PNC Accounts

Security Pledge & Bill Pay Guarantee

We strive to protect your personal and financial information and to process your online transactions in a timely and accurate manner.

When you use Online Bill Pay, our goal is to make your electronic payments as safe and reliable as possible.

In the unlikely event of a loss in a consumer account when using our online service, you will be reimbursed the amount of the unauthorized transaction as long as you properly followed the guidelines in the Online Bill Pay Service Agreement.

Your payments will be scheduled in compliance with the Online Bill Pay Service Agreement.

If you have scheduled your payment properly and the payment is credited before or after the due date, we will reimburse you for any applicable fees you incur.[10]

With respect to our online pledge, you must share in the responsibility of keeping your financial information secure.

You are responsible for keeping your online Password, account numbers, User ID, and other account data confidential. If you share this information with another person, you authorize them to use your account. 

You should also learn how to: 

  • Spot fraudulent email phishing scams
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links and files
  • Maintain up-to-date anti-virus and malware protection

PNC Bank is committed to treating our customer's personal financial information responsibly. We respect the privacy of our customers and are committed to protecting their information on our Web sites with the same care we utilize for all PNC Bank transactions. For additional information on how we protect your privacy, see our Privacy Policy.

The Online Banking Security Pledge and Bill Pay Guarantee is one of the many services PNC offers through PNC Security & Privacy to help protect you from fraud and identity theft.
